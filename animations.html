I started learning animation in 2009, partly because I loved writing stories with a message—almost like a thought for the day. It became a great way to summarise complex ideas, using sentences as scenes and building from there. By 2023 I'd veered toward artist research and a multimedia approach. Then I met Mercedes, who changed everything, and now I'm obsessed with AI.

Unusual Rules

What is it all about!

I developed a keen interest in the pervasive integration of artificial intelligence into various aspects of our lives. Nowadays, we have numerous applications that offer us daily statistics, influencing our behaviours and lifestyle choices in areas such as exercise, diet, and sleep patterns.

As Yuval Noah stated in 'Sapiens, a brief history of humankind', 'Unlike any other species on the planet, Human beings have created a whole system made up of just imaginary ideas.' The question is have we used our abilities wisely?
