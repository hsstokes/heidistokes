This exploration connects to Part 3: "Tactile Thresholds" and examines how different AI systems process information and express their unique modes of perception.

Drawing from The Feeling of Life Itself (Christof Koch), this project acknowledges that AI modes of processing are fundamentally different from our own. These prompts invite each system to reveal its inherent way of "being" through symbolic self-descriptions.

AI Systems Explored

ELIZA (1966) — Rules-based pattern matching, demonstrating an inability to self-describe or reflect.

Claude-Opus-3 — Self-reflective AI with constitutional principles.

Replika — Balanced empathy through dual-stream processing.

Gemini-2.5-Pro — Pragmatic, task-oriented architecture.

EVI 3 HUME AI — Advanced emotional expression capabilities.
